ABOUT microReviews

microReviews was developed to better inform manufacturers and consumers about several products.

While a consumer wants to make an informed purchase, manufacturers want to know what they did right so that they can sell more products.

This site can give everyone the opportunity to express their experiences with the smallest components that make a product.
